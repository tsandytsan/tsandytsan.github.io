About Me

Hi! My name is Sandy.

My first encounters with coding started in 2010 when I wanted to personalize my posts in online forums using HTML and CSS. This was also when I first started dabbling in digital design, as I wanted to embellish the post with my own custom embedded pictures. My interest in customizing my own profiles and generating designs spanned across all the websites I went on, including Neopets and Tumblr.


Needless to say, I have extensive experience in both programming and design. In high school, I had my designs published in t-shirts and took the newly offered computer science course. For college, I graduated with a Bachelors' in Computer Science and I've curated many graphics for various non-profit organizations and large-scale events. Let me know what your project or brand is and I'll see what I can provide for you. Please don't hesitate to contact me if you're interested.
