Terminalgram

Made with: Hadoop DFS, PostgreSQL, Java


An Instagram like application with a terminal-style menu to navigate the app. The system allows users to add a post, follow other users, view other posts from people they follow and view a news feed of the top photos based on the people they follow. Searches can be made to view users based on their username and tags. Users can also search photos based on tags and users. There is also an option to list the most popular photos and users in the application.

Pet Simulator

Made with: Arduino UNO R3, C for ATmega1284


Pet Simulator is an interactive game where the main goal is to take care of your pet, taking inspirations from Bandai's Tamagotchi of the late 1990s. In this game, players can feed, play, and wash your virtual friend in order to maintain its health bar. ________, there is also an option to kick the pet in order to decrease the health bar.
